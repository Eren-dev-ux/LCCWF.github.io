Our Story

Lighthouse Children and Community Wellness Foundation (LCCWF) is a non-profit, non-governmental organization committed to improving the well-being of vulnerable children and strengthening the health and resilience of underserved communities.

Founded on the principles of compassion, equity, and sustainable development, the Foundation serves as a beacon of hopeâ€”guiding individuals and families toward healthier, safer, and more empowered lives.

We work collaboratively with communities, local authorities, and development partners to address critical social challenges affecting children and families, particularly in marginalized and resource-limited settings.

Our Vision

A society where every child is protected, healthy, educated, and empowered, and where communities thrive in dignity, wellness, and self-reliance.

Our Mission

To promote the holistic development and protection of children while enhancing community wellness through integrated programs in health, education, psychosocial support, and sustainable livelihoods.

Our Core Values

Child-Centered Approach

Prioritizing the rights, safety, and well-being of children in all our interventions.

Equity & Inclusion

Serving all people without discrimination, ensuring equal access to opportunities.

Community Ownership

Empowering communities to lead their own development and sustain initiatives.

Integrity & Accountability

Upholding transparency, ethical practices, and responsible stewardship of resources.

Collaboration

Partnering with stakeholders for greater impact and sustainable change.

Sustainability

Designing long-term, impactful solutions that create lasting positive change.

Governance & Leadership

Board of Trustees

The Foundation is governed by a Board of Trustees responsible for strategic oversight, policy direction, and accountability. Our board members bring diverse expertise in child welfare, public health, education, and community development.

Management Team

Day-to-day operations are managed by an experienced leadership team supported by dedicated program staff, field officers, volunteers, and community facilitators.

Transparency & Accountability

We maintain the highest standards of transparency through regular financial audits, annual reports, and open communication with stakeholders.
